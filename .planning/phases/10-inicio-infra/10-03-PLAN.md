  <decision>Pick the custom domain strategy for Tikin Dashboard</decision>
  <context>
    INFRA-04 has been open since v1.0 Phase 5. The dashboard currently lives at a Vercel-generated `*.vercel.app` URL with Vercel Deployment Protection (SSO required). Mitigation has always been "configure custom domain → bypasses SSO". Three options on the table:
  </context>
  <options>
    <option id="subdomain">
      <name>Subdomain (e.g. dashboard.tikin.co)</name>
      <pros>Lowest friction; one CNAME record at the DNS provider; Vercel auto-provisions SSL; doesn't disrupt the apex domain or other subdomains; reversible at zero cost</pros>
      <cons>Requires `tikin.co` (or whatever apex you control) to already be live with a working DNS zone. URL is slightly longer than the apex would be.</cons>
    </option>
    <option id="apex">
      <name>Apex / root domain (e.g. tikin.co or another bare domain)</name>
      <pros>Cleanest URL ("dashboard.tikin.co" is the typical answer here BUT if you specifically want the root-domain experience and you control that apex…); same Vercel SSL provisioning</pros>
      <cons>Apex requires an A record (76.76.21.21) plus a CNAME for `www` — slightly more DNS surface; conflicts with any existing apex usage (marketing site, email MX, etc.) — needs verification that no other service is bound; reversible but may take longer to fully propagate</cons>
    </option>
    <option id="defer">
      <name>Defer INFRA-04 again</name>
      <pros>Zero immediate work; v2.0 functional milestone (Inicio v2 in Plan 10-02) ships independently; cliente-foco share URL still works on `*.vercel.app` (with SSO challenge — operator-mediated for now)</pros>
      <cons>v2.0 milestone closes with a documented infra debt; cliente-foco UX is degraded (cliente sees Vercel SSO page first); blocker carries to v3.0 or whenever the next milestone surfaces it</cons>
    </option>
  </options>
